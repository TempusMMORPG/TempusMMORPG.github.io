Tempus Region Gym Leaders

Reid - Normal Gym Leader

Location:

La Relance

Biography:

Reid was an apprenticeship working under the La Relance gym. After three years, the former gym leader suddenly went radio silent (coincidentally right when Reid was graduating) due to getting tired and old but not being able to find an external successor that fits the League's requirements.

The League had difficulty selecting someone to fill the position, but were aware that there was an apprentice at the gym who has the experience and the unlikelihood they would leave anytime soon.

Thus, Reid was offered the position upon graduation and has been working there ever since. Reid accepted the offer as it allowed them to pursue their knowledge and growth without the needless worry of additional responsibilities.

Overall, Reid is analytical and has a rude and selfish personality.

Gym Affiliation:

Normal Gym

Ace Pokemon:

Ambipom

Capt. Atlas - Flying Gym Leader

Location:

Venola

Biography:

A veteran air force pilot from Venola who became a commercial pilot in Tempus after his retirement. He is well known for his sharp decision-making and attention to detail while on the job and in battle. His ace is Swellow, the partner he had while in the Air Force.

Gym Affiliation:

Flying Gym

Ace Pokemon:

Swellow
